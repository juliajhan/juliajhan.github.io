About Me

Hi, I'm Julia, I'm a passionate software engineer and a proud first-generation female Korean immigrant with a keen interest in Web Development. With a strong foundation in Python, Java, TypeScript, JavaScript, and Node.js, I thrive on building innovative solutions and solving complex problems.

I thrive on building innovative solutions that make a meaningful impact. Whether it’s developing user-friendly applications or solving complex problems, I approach each project with a growth mindset and a focus on continuous improvement. In my recent work, I've focused on creating mobile apps, and I am always eager to take on new challenges.

As a first-generation immigrant, I bring a unique perspective to everything I do, from my problem-solving approach to my dedication to learning and growing in the tech field. Outside of coding, I enjoy fitness, volunteering for animal shelters and churches, working on side projects, and crocheting.

I am excited to connect with like-minded professionals and continue growing in this ever-evolving field!

Projects

Dined

Dined is a mobile app designed to streamline your decision-making process when it comes to choosing where to eat. Whether you're craving a new dining experience or just can't decide between options, Dined makes it easy to explore local restaurants and swipe through choices to find your next favorite meal. With an intuitive interface and an engaging swipe feature, you can quickly browse nearby restaurants, make informed decisions, and discover new places to enjoy with friends or family. Say goodbye to the endless "What should we eat?" dilemma, and let Dined help you make dining decisions fun and effortless.

Dalendar

Dalendar is a comprehensive scheduling app that helps you take control of your time and health. Designed with a special focus on tracking period cycles, Dalendar allows you to easily monitor your menstrual health and predict future cycles with accuracy. Alongside period tracking, the app offers seamless scheduling for appointments, events, and reminders, making it your all-in-one solution for organizing your personal and health-related activities. With intuitive charts, customizable reminders, and an easy-to-use interface, Dalendar empowers you to stay on top of your cycle and daily schedule, helping you make informed decisions about your health and well-being.
